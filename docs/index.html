Founder Story

I am Sneha Mali, and Seere Yaana is my love letter to heritage.

I am an MBA graduate, a proud proprietor, and a creative soul who finds joy in cooking, dancing, and making something beautiful from everyday inspiration. My love for sarees and contemporary art gave birth to Seere Yaana.

This venture is deeply personal to me. I hold my ancestral heritage close, and I express it with a fresh, elegant twist for the women of today. Every drape I curate carries a story of roots, rhythm, and reinvention.
